The Little Flower’s School Trust is in the field of Education for over 90 years. The Little Flower's College of Information Technology ( Degree College ) is a part of The Little Flower’s School Trust. The Little Flower's College of Information Technology ( Degree College ) was started in the year 2000. Though the degree courses are conducted through regular mode, there are regular classes from Monday to Friday similar to that of a regular college. The students who are working can also register for these courses through our weekend batches.
